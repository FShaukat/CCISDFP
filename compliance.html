Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is included in the federal report cards?
TEA’s federal report cards include data on STAAR performance, graduation rates, English proficiency (TELPAS), school quality/student success, civil rights indicators, and per-pupil expenditures :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}.
How do I access the District Improvement Plan?
The DIP PDF is linked above and is updated annually to ensure compliance with TEC §11.252, reflecting goals and performance strategies aligned with board-approved strategic plans :contentReference[oaicite:5]{index=5}.
Who to contact for help understanding forms or timelines?

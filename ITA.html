Intent to Apply

If you are interested in collaborating on a grant that is currently open, please complete the form below to notify the Grants Office of your intent. This helps us coordinate district-wide efforts, provide timely support, and ensure all grant applications meet internal and external requirements.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Do I need approval before submitting this form?
No. This form simply alerts the Grants Office of your interest. You’ll receive follow-up regarding approvals or next steps.
What happens after I submit?
You will be contacted to schedule a brief planning meeting or provided with guidance depending on the nature of the opportunity.
Is this form for TEA grants only?
No — it’s for any competitive or discretionary grant you are interested in pursuing with CCISD support.

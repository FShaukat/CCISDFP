Federal Programs

The CCISD Federal Programs Department empowers student success by ensuring every eligible student receives high-quality educational support through federally funded services and programs. We manage both compliance-based and competitive funding sources that strengthen academic equity and drive innovation across our district.

We work in partnership with schools, families, and community organizations to expand access, promote transparency, and maximize the impact of every federal dollar.

What We Do

Our department oversees a wide range of federal initiatives that support:

  • Academic achievement for students in Title I schools
  • Language instruction for English learners and immigrant students
  • Resources for students experiencing homelessness
  • Parental engagement in learning
  • Strategic grant opportunities for schools and educators

We also ensure full compliance with the Every Student Succeeds Act (ESSA) and related state and federal regulations.

Our Mission

To provide leadership and oversight of federal programs that promote equity, access, and excellence in education, while maintaining strong accountability, family engagement, and support for CCISD schools.

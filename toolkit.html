Why use a logic model?

Logic models show funders how your project works: starting with resources, listing what you'll do, showing what those activities produce, and demonstrating how they lead to real outcomes. They help clarify your plan and build confidence in your work.

What should I do first when starting a grant application?

Start by clearly defining your project idea. What problem are you trying to solve? Who will benefit? Then use the Program Planning Packet to map out goals, activities, and timeline before writing. Remember to submit the CCISD Intent to Apply form before beginning any draft!

How detailed should my project timeline be?

Your timeline should identify key milestones, deadlines, and deliverables, including start/end dates and reporting periods. Funders want to see that your plan is realistic and actionable ‚Äî even if there‚Äôs flexibility built in.

Do I need to include evaluation measures even for small grants?

Yes ‚Äî almost all grants require some evidence that your project achieved its goals. Even a simple evaluation plan (e.g., pre/post surveys or tracking participation numbers) shows you can demonstrate impact and success.

What is the difference between goals and objectives?

Goals are broad, general statements about what you hope to achieve (e.g., improve literacy). Objectives are specific, measurable steps that support those goals (e.g., 85% of students will increase reading comprehension scores by 10%). Objectives should be SMART: Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, and Time-bound.

What is a proposal checklist for?

The proposal checklist helps ensure your grant package is complete before submission ‚Äî confirming that you‚Äôve followed all instructions, attached required documents, and addressed evaluation criteria. It prevents simple omissions that could disqualify your proposal.

What should I include in my project budget?

Your budget should reflect all anticipated project costs, including personnel, supplies, travel, equipment, and indirect costs if allowed. Make sure every line item is clearly justified and tied to your project‚Äôs objectives and activities.

Can I use these templates for foundation or corporate grants too?

Yes ‚Äî while some templates reference federal grant requirements, the tools (like the logic model and timeline template) work for all types of grants. Private funders also appreciate clear plans, strong objectives, and evidence of evaluation readiness.

Why do funders care so much about logic models?

Logic models show that your project is thoughtful, coherent, and measurable. They help reviewers quickly understand what you‚Äôre trying to accomplish, why it matters, and how you‚Äôll know it worked ‚Äî all in one diagram.

Do I have to submit the Intent to Apply form even for small grants?

Yes ‚Äî the Intent to Apply process helps ensure that all CCISD grants align with district priorities and that necessary approvals and support are in place. It also gives the Grants Office an opportunity to help strengthen your application, regardless of the grant size.

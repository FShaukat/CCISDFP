Family Engagement

Purpose & Compliance

CCISD supports meaningful family engagement as required under Title I, Part A of the Every Student Succeeds Act (ESSA). Our Family & Community Engagement efforts are designed to strengthen partnerships between schools and families to boost student learning and success. We promote two-way communication, shared responsibility, and culturally responsive collaboration between schools and parents.

Written Plans & Annual Review

Each Title I campus develops a Parent & Family Engagement Plan in collaboration with families. These plans are:

  • Reviewed and revised annually with parent input
  • Shared in understandable and parent-preferred languages
  • Distributed via campus websites and newsletters

Additionally, all campuses provide a School–Parent Compact outlining shared responsibilities for student achievement.

Everyday Engagement Strategies

CCISD Title I schools implement regular and practical methods for involving families, including:

  • Parent–teacher conferences focused on academic progress and student goals
  • Interactive events such as Literacy, Math, and STEM Nights
  • Daily folders and communication apps for consistent parent updates
  • Resources sent home to reinforce learning

Specialized Family Supports

To meet diverse family needs, CCISD provides:

  • Grand Learning Workshops: Resources and support for grandparents raising grandchildren
  • Bilingual Tech Trainings: Sessions on using Skyward and other district tools
  • ReadyRosie: A free digital platform offering brief videos and learning activities for children ages 0–5

Evaluation & Feedback

Each year, we conduct a district-wide Family Engagement Survey and campus-level evaluations. Results are used to revise campus plans and inform professional development for staff. Families are invited to share feedback on events and communication through surveys and campus meetings.

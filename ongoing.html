Cybersecurity Mitigation Grant

CCISD is preparing to launch the Cybersecurity Mitigation Grant awarded by the Texas Office of the Governor. This initiative will enhance the district’s cybersecurity posture and infrastructure to meet current state and federal standards.

State & Local Cybersecurity Grant Program (SLCGP)

Status: Awarded — Implementation Begins FY 2024

CCISD will strengthen digital protections through multi-factor authentication, data encryption, system backups, staff training, and improved governance under the SLCGP. This is part of a broader statewide initiative to enhance local education agencies' cyber resilience.

Key Objectives:

  • Enable multi-factor authentication and encryption for critical systems
  • Improve data backup systems and address hardware/software vulnerabilities
  • Advance district cybersecurity planning and policy frameworks
  • Deliver incident response training to IT and operations staff

Grant Period: November 1, 2024 – October 31, 2025

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What systems are being protected?
This grant focuses on securing staff access, student information systems, servers, and critical network infrastructure.
Will there be cybersecurity training for staff?
Yes — the grant funds training for district-level IT staff and strategic planning teams.
What is the match requirement?
A 20% local match is required. CCISD has budgeted for this and no contributions are required from campuses.

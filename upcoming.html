Upcoming Grant‑Funded Initiatives at CCISD

Clear Creek ISD is excited to share several initiatives that have been awarded through competitive grants. These programs will launch over the next two school years and support innovation, professional learning, and equitable access across our campuses.

TEA Strategic Staffing Grant

Status: Funded for 2025–2027

This two-year grant supports CCISD in launching paid teacher residencies through partnerships with educator preparation programs, aimed at building long-term teacher pipelines in high-need areas.

Learn More at TEA »

Interactive Online Learning Grant (Newsela)

Status: Funded for 2025–2027 (Cycle 2)

This grant enables CCISD to implement Newsela’s TEKS-aligned interactive learning platform in grades 7–8 social studies—complete with bilingual resources, embedded media, and professional development support.

View Cycle 2 Grant Details »

Empowering ECSE Educators Grant (Region 10 ESC)

Status: Participation confirmed for 2024–2025

Through this grant, CCISD’s early childhood educators will receive training and certification in Conscious Discipline and inclusive ECSE practices, enhancing classroom support for our youngest learners.

Visit Region 10 ESC Program Page »

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Can individual teachers apply for these grants?
No. These grants were submitted and awarded to CCISD. Participation is coordinated by your campus or department.
How will I know if my campus is involved?
Your campus administration will share details about participation, training, and roll-out timelines.
Can I propose a different grant initiative?
Absolutely! Please visit the “Submit a Grant Wishlist” or “Request Grant Support” sections of the Grants Portal to share your ideas or ask for help.
